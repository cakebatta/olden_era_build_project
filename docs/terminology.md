# Terminology

## SID
Stable internal identifier used by the game.

## Unit Family
A base creature plus its two upgrade options.

## Building Level
A specific upgrade level of a building. Each level is treated as a unique node.

## Dependency Graph
Directed graph describing prerequisite relationships.

## Topological Order
Any legal construction sequence satisfying all prerequisites.

## Build Plan
A dated sequence of construction actions with cumulative costs.

## Connected Database
Combined city, unit, and localization data loaded into memory.
